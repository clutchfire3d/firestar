## 8. Step-by-Step Assembly

### Step 1: Lower Receiver Preparation
* **Trigger:** Install the trigger blade. Note the specific slot to prevent the selector from over-rotating. Secure with an M3 screw.
![Trigger Install](Pages/Images/step1_1.jpg)
* **Grip Inserts:** Using a soldering iron, install the M3 brass inserts into the Pistol Grip mounting holes.
![Grip Inserts](Pages/Images/step1_2.jpg)
* **Grip:** Bolt the Pistol Grip to the body using M3 screws.
![Grip Mount](Pages/Images/step1_3.jpg)
* **Mag Release:** Install the printed (or aftermarket M4) Mag Release with the spring. Ensure smooth movement.
![Mag Release](Pages/Images/step1_4.jpg)

### Step 2: Upper Receiver & Handguard
* **Inserts:** Install M3 inserts into both sides of the Upper Receiver and the Outer Barrel.
![Upper Inserts](Pages/Images/step2_1.jpg)
* **Muzzle:** Screw the 14mm CCW adapter onto the Outer Barrel tip.
![Muzzle Thread](Pages/Images/step2_2.jpg)
* **Alignment:** Align the Outer Barrel holes with the Handguard. Pay attention to the **chamfered side**.
![Barrel Alignment](Pages/Images/step2_3.jpg)
* **Joining:** Slide the Upper into the Handguard and secure both sides with M3 screws.
![Upper Handguard Join](Pages/Images/step2_4.jpg)
* **Hop-up:** Slide the assembled Hop-up and inner barrel into the Upper until it hits the internal limiter.
![Hopup Install](Pages/Images/step2_5.jpg)

### Step 3: Trigger Housing & HPA
* **Trigger Board:** Fix the F2 trigger board to the left side of the Housing using the original small screw.
![Trigger Board](Pages/Images/step3_1.jpg)
* **Assembly:** Place the trigger blade in its housing, close it with the right side, and secure with M3 screws.
![Housing Close](Pages/Images/step3_2.jpg)
* **Selector:** Snap the Selector Plate into place.
![Selector Plate](Pages/Images/step3_3.jpg)

### Step 4: Stock Adapter
* **Nut Setup:** Place the M5 nut inside the specific slot within the Lower Receiver.
![M5 Nut](Pages/Images/step4_1.jpg)
* **Fixation:** Install the adapter before the HPA system and secure it with the M5 screw.
![Adapter Mount](Pages/Images/step4_2.jpg)

### Step 5: Final System Assembly
* **Wiring:** Route the FCU wires through the Stock Adapter and the air line through the Pistol Grip hole.
![Wiring Route](Pages/Images/step5_1.jpg)
* **Trigger Box:** Slide the Trigger Housing into the Lower and lock it with the **Lower Pin**.
![Trigger Box Install](Pages/Images/step5_2.jpg)
* **Engine:** Slide the F2 engine into the Upper until it seats into the Hop-up.
![Engine Slide](Pages/Images/step5_3.jpg)
* **Lock:** Install the **F2 Lock** to prevent the engine from moving during shots.
![Engine Lock](Pages/Images/step5_4.jpg)
* **Closing:** Join the Upper and Lower receivers and lock with the front pin.
![Body Pins](Pages/Images/step5_5.jpg)

### Step 6: Stock (Coronha)
* **Carbon Tube:** Cut the carbon fiber tube to length and drill the mounting holes.
![Tube Prep](Pages/Images/step6_1.jpg)
* **Stock:** Install M3 inserts into the printed Stock part and bolt it to the tube.
![Stock Mount](Pages/Images/step6_2.jpg)
* **Finalize:** Connect the FCU and battery, tuck the wires into the tube, and slide it onto the Adapter.
![Final Wiring](Pages/Images/step6_3.jpg)
