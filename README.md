## 1. Meet Firestar
**Forget Gearboxes. Embrace Innovation.**

![Firestar](Pages/Images/Firestar.jpg)

The Firestar was developed by **ClutchFire3D** leveraging **PolarStar Airsoft** technology. Designed from the ground up to extract maximum performance from the **F2‚Ñ¢ and Jack‚Ñ¢ HPA engines**, this platform eliminates the limitations of adapted AEG bodies.

### What makes Firestar unique:
* ‚öôÔ∏è **Native HPA (No Gearbox):** Designed specifically for HPA, eliminating the gearbox shell.
* üéØ **Perfect Alignment:** Alignment is guaranteed by the native design.
* ‚öñÔ∏è **Ultralight:** Weighing approximately 600g (depending on setup).
* üõ°Ô∏è **Robust Geometry:** Optimized for engineering-grade materials (ABS/ASA).
* üé® **Total Freedom (DIY):** Compatible with standard M4-style Pistol Grips, Mag Catches, and Stocks.

# Step 1: Lower Receiver Preparation

### **Trigger:** Install the trigger blade. Note the specific slot to prevent the selector from over-rotating. Secure with an M3 screw.
![Trigger Install](Pages/Images/img1.jpg)

### **Grip Inserts & Mounting:** Using a soldering iron, install the M3 brass inserts into the Pistol Grip mounting holes. Then, bolt the Pistol Grip to the body using M3 screws.

# Step 2: Upper Receiver & Handguard

### **Inserts & Muzzle:** Install M3 inserts into both sides of the Upper Receiver and the Outer Barrel. Screw the 14mm CCW adapter onto the Outer Barrel tip.

| Inserts | Muzzle Thread |
| :---: | :---: |
| ![Upper Inserts](Pages/Images/img6.jpg) | ![Muzzle Thread](Pages/Images/img7.jpg) |

### **Alignment & Joining:** Align the Outer Barrel holes with the Handguard (pay attention to the **chamfered side**). Slide the Upper into the Handguard and secure both sides with M3 screws.

![Barrel Alignment](Pages/Images/img8.jpg)

### **Hop-up:** Slide the assembled Hop-up and inner barrel into the Upper until it hits the internal limiter.
![Hopup Install](Pages/Images/img12.jpg)

# Step 3: Trigger Housing & HPA

### **Trigger Board:** Fix the F2 trigger board to the left side of the Housing using the original small screw.
![Trigger Board](Pages/Images/img14.jpg)

### **Final Housing Assembly:** Place the trigger blade in its housing, close it with the right side, secure with M3 screws, and snap the Selector Plate into place.

# Step 4: Stock Adapter

### **Nut & Fixation:** Place the M5 nut inside the specific slot within the Lower Receiver. Install the Stock Adapter before the HPA system and secure it with the M5 screw.

| M5 Nut | Adapter Mount |
| :---: | :---: |
| ![M5 Nut](Pages/Images/img19.jpg) | ![Adapter Mount](Pages/Images/img20.jpg) |

---

# Step 5: Final System Assembly

1. **Wiring:** Route FCU wires through the Stock Adapter and air line through the Pistol Grip.
2. **Trigger Box:** Slide Housing into the Lower and lock with the **Lower Pin**.
3. **Engine:** Slide the F2 engine into the Upper until it seats into the Hop-up.
4. **Lock:** Install the **F2 Lock**.
5. **Closing:** Join Upper/Lower receivers and lock with the front pin.

---

# Step 6: Stock

### **Finalizing the Build:** Cut the carbon fiber tube, drill holes, and bolt the printed stock to the tube using M3 inserts. Connect the FCU and battery, tucking them into the tube.
